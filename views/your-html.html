Ibiza style guide 2018

Everyone’s favourite party island is always the biggest inspo for holiday style, and if you’re heading to the White Isle for closing parties that might mean you’re feeling the packing pressure. How many bikinis do you really need for a long weekend? What should you wear for day drinking on a boat? And how can you make sure to turn heads at Pacha? Whether you’re hitting the clubs hard, or posing by the pool all day, we’ve got the perfect Ibiza outfits for you.

Pool party

If you don’t go to at least one pool party have you even been to Ibiza? Whether you’re heading to Ocean Beach for the ultimate pool party experience, or your hotel is throwing its own poolside bash, you need to nail your look. This event is for showing off that knockout swimwear you don’t even want to get wet. Strappy bandage bikinis and cutout swimsuits are perfect – Extra points for tan-enhancing metallics. Throw a kimono cover up on to keep the chill off when the sun goes down, and you have pool party #outfitgoals.

Pacha

So we doubt you’re flying to Ibiza to catch up on your sleep. Everybody knows the White Isle comes alive when the sun goes down, and if you’re off to Ibiza for closing parties then you’re heading to Pacha, Amnesia, or Ushaia (maybe all 3 if you’re dedicated enough). A bodycon dress is always a safe bet for showing off your curves. For an iconic Ibiza clubbing look that’s right up to date, go for a dress with cutouts, mesh, crochet or prints. Wear with flats or heels depending on the venue vibes (and whether you’re planning on dancing solidly until sunrise).

Glitterbox

A legendary closing night on the Ibiza clubbing scene, this is a chance to get your glitter ON. Nothing is too OTT for Glitterbox, so go all out if you want to turn heads. Metallic and sequinned dresses are obvious choices, and perfect instant outfits which don’t take up too much room in your suitcase. If you don’t want to go full disco ball, wear one embellished piece (like a beaded skirt or sequinned top) in an otherwise plain outfit. Don’t be afraid to accessorise with body jewels, and glitter makeup. Shine bright like a diamond, babes.

Boat party

What’s better than sailing around Ibiza on a boat? Sailing around Ibiza on a boat with a bar, of course. Partying in the middle of the Mediterranean is a rite of passage for many Ibiza partygoers, and nailing your boat party outfit is essential. Swimwear is a must, obvs, and you’re going to want something that’ll have you looking hot but that’s practical for diving off the boat when you’re ready for some vitamin sea. Cover up with some frayed denim shorts and a crop top for a casj but cute boat party outfit.
